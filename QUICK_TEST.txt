✅ STEP 1: BUILD APK (3-5 minutes)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

In Android Studio:
□ Build → Build Bundle(s) / APK(s) → Build APK(s)
□ Wait for "APK(s) generated successfully"
□ Click "locate"

APK at: C:\Users\Chris\Documents\Gastotrack\mobile\android\app\build\outputs\apk\debug\app-debug.apk

✅ STEP 2: INSTALL ON PHONE (2 minutes)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

□ Connect phone to PC via USB
□ Copy app-debug.apk to phone's Downloads
□ On phone: Files → Downloads → Tap app-debug.apk
□ Tap "Install"
□ Tap "Open"

✅ STEP 3: SETUP (1 minute)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

□ Phone connected to same WiFi as PC
□ Backend running: php artisan serve
□ Test in phone browser: http://192.168.0.11:8000
   (Should see Laravel page)
